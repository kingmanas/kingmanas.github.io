06 — FOUNDER NOTE [ MANIFESTO ]
Why I started
BLDR.

When I started building during my second year, I realised something quickly:

ambitious people are everywhere —
but most of them are building alone.

Everyone around us was preparing for predictable paths,

while a small group of people were trying to create something of their own.

Startups. Projects. Communities. Businesses. Ideas.

BLDR started as an attempt to
bring those people together.

Not the people who already made it.

The people still trying. The people still building.

— FOUNDER, BLDR
